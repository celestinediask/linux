File naming format

no caps (URL direct to diffrent address)
no space (URL add %20)
no special character (hard to read and OS dont allow)
no underscore (URL breaks)
no period (.)

Year format: YYYYMMDD
Time format: HHMMSS

Be consistent

no more than 50 letters

limit path upto 10

Eg. boo-backup-20220423-232454
